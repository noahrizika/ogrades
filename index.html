About Me

My name is Noah Rizika, and I'm a senior at Middlebury College in Vermont. I'll be graduating with a degree in biochemistry, and after deciding against medical and biochemical research careers, I'm pursuing my other interest: coding! I'm looking to combine my medical and chemical knowledge into a career in creating embedded medical device software.

Before pursing a career in programming, I've had a variety of experiences, ranging from leading groups on multi-day backcountry trips to assisting in neuroscience research. Orchestrating my own computer science education, I've taken some of Middlebury's courses, such as Computer Architecture and a data analytics course, and many more online courses from coursera, Udemy and Udacity. Most notably, I've earned Udacity's Nanodegree in Machine Learning with AWS.
